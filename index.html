About me

In December 2023, I joined CeADAR Ireland's Centre For Applied AI as a data scientist to work on model efficiency and trustworthiness for multiple modalities of data. Our work includes compression of NN-based models, domain adaptation, and data generation, amongs other areas.

Previously, during my postdoctoral position at Insight SFI Centre for Data Analytics at Dublin City University in Ireland, I focused on applying and developing AI techniques. This work spanned a various topics including time series analysis and computer vision for video data and precision farming, computer vision applied to medical imaging, and traditional machine learning techniques for water quality analysis.

During my PhD, I studied the training of neural networks under supervision and computation constraints, developing algorithms to address key limitations in the field. I received my B.E. and M.E. in Telecommunications Engineering from Universitat Polit√®cnica de Catalunya in Barcelona.

My primary research interest is the application and development of machine learning techniques to enhance AI systems' understanding and interaction with the world, with a particular focus on building trustworthy and reliable AI solutions.
